# Vinda Store Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior and sales patterns for the Vinda Store to identify key demographics, top-performing regions, and high-impact marketing channels. The goal is to provide actionable insights to improve future sales performance.

## Key Insights

### 1. Customer Gender
- Women are more likely to make purchases compared to men.
- Female purchase share: ~65%

### 2. Top Performing States
The highest sales contribution comes from:
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Uttar Pradesh

### 3. Age Group Contribution
- The Adult age group (30 to 49 years) forms the largest segment.
- This group contributes approximately 50% of total sales.

### 4. Top Sales Channels
Most purchases come from:
- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra

## Final Conclusion
To improve Vinda Store sales, focus marketing efforts on:

Target Audience:
- Women
- Ages 30 to 49 years
- Living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh

Recommended Strategy:
- Promote through Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra using:
  - Targeted ads
  - Exclusive deals
  - Digital coupons
  - Limited-time offers
